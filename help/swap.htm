swap

 

Description

 

Swap any subset of the following characteristics of a g-blink:

 

(FV) - face-edges with vertex-edges (default = 0)

(P)  - the parity of the vertices (default = 0)

(C)  - undercross/overcross state of the zigzag-edges (default = 1)

 

Examples

 

Usage

 

swap(gblinks)

 

swap the crossings: undercross/overcross state of zigzag-edges

 

swap(gblinks,P=1,FV=1,C=1)

 

swap all three traits of the g-blink(s).
